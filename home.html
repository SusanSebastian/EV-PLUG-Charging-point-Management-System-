About EVPLUG


Future is here

The EVPLUG was founded by Ms.Susan Sebastian Head of Automotive Kerala. The aim of EVPLUG is to bring the EV users to a group where they can pre-book the station and plan their journey accordingly. This helps the people to efficiently use the electric vehicles without fearing the range and battery capacity

An electric vehicle charging station is equipment that connects an electric vehicle (EV) to a source of electricity to recharge electric cars, neighborhood electric vehicles and plug-in hybrids.
